Lemon Drop Shot

Description

A lemon drop shot is a yummy alcoholic vodka-based shot that's perfect for parties. It tastes just like the classic lemon drop candy!

Ingredients

  • Vodka: You can use whatever vodka variety you have on hand.
  • Lemon: Youâ€™ll need fresh lemon juice and lemon wedges.
  • Sugar: White sugar perfectly balances the tart lemon flavor.

Steps

  1. Combine the vodka and juice in a shot glass.
  2. Sprinkle the shot with sugar.
  3. Dip the lemon wedges in sugar, then use the wedges as a chaser.
